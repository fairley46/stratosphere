# Stratosphere Glossary

Plain-language terms for non-technical application owners.

## Current-State Map
- Meaning: A picture of how your application works today.
- Why it matters: Shows what exists now before any change is proposed.

## Future-State Map
- Meaning: A picture of how Stratosphere recommends running the app after modernization.
- Why it matters: Helps compare today vs tomorrow in one view.

## Readiness Score
- Meaning: A confidence score for how prepared the migration plan is.
- Why it matters: Shows if key information is missing before cutover work begins.

## Unknowns
- Meaning: Questions that still need human answers.
- Why it matters: Unknowns increase risk and should be resolved before deployment.

## Blue/Green Migration
- Meaning: Keeping the old app running while a new version is validated.
- Why it matters: Reduces outage risk and supports safer rollback.

## Rollback
- Meaning: Switching traffic back to the previous stable environment.
- Why it matters: Protects the business if something goes wrong during cutover.

## Stateful Workload
- Meaning: A component that keeps important stored data.
- Why it matters: Needs extra migration care to avoid data loss or corruption.

## Human Sign-Off
- Meaning: A required approval from accountable people before execution.
- Why it matters: Prevents accidental or unreviewed production changes.
